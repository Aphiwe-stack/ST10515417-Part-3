WEBSITE CHOICE     

Business Name: Mechanical Solutions   

Description      

Mechanical Solutions is a NGO based website. The purpose of this website is to help people with identifying complicated parts of a certain vehicle or any machinery. This website solves vehicle problems with the help of Google tools, as some things are very difficult for a human mind to understand in some cases of emergency. I built this website for people who are into mechanical structures and people who would like to learn more about different machineries. 

Reasons Why I Chose This Website/Organization: 

I chose to create this website/organization because I believe that it is a creative idea and it can play a huge role for many more years to come especially since machinery is used in  every sector nowadays. I chose this organization because this website is a non-profit organization that operates outside of the government. I created this platform so that people who have no knowledge in mechanical parts can use my website to easily identify every part in a vehicle or machine, with the help of Mechanical Solutions, it is easy for users to identify parts accurately. After the platform has analyzed your requested information on a certain mechanical part the name and the purpose of the part is then displayed to the user. I made this website/business to educate people on the structures of machinery and mechanical parts, I made this website for enthusiastic and curious people.
